Four Novel Algorithms
Novel Contribution 01
ST-DBSCAN Adaptive Clustering
Extends Birant-Kut ST-DBSCAN with dynamic spatial radius ε calibrated by Census 2021 population density class — collapses to 200m at max density, expands to 800m at min density.
ε(density_class) = {1:800m, 2:600m, 3:400m, 4:300m, 5:200m}
T_window = 72h · MinPts = 3
Birant & Kut (2007) · Data & Knowledge Engineering 60(1)
Novel Contribution 02
Causal Inference Graph Engine
Bayesian knowledge graph of 8 infrastructure failure signatures. Each cluster is scored against all signatures — officer dispatch only when confidence exceeds 84% threshold.
8 signatures · Confidence threshold > 0.84
Below threshold → "unclassified" → manual review
Applied to civic data — novel implementation · Neo4j Cypher
Novel Contribution 03
Environmental Decay Cost Model
Converts unresolved complaints into real-time financial liability. Infrastructure damage cost grows exponentially — dengue risk escalates after day 7 (Aedes aegypti larval cycle).
C(t) = [C_repair × e^(λt)] + Σ(αᵢ × Hᵢ(t) × P_density)
λ=0.08 waterlogging · dengue critical day 14+
Infrastructure decay literature · NDMC + IMD + CPCB public data
Novel Contribution 04
Environmental ROI Budget Optimizer
Replaces complaint-volume prioritisation with environmental return on investment. Greedy 0/1 knapsack approximation — within 15% of optimal, O(n log n) time.
Maximise: Σ(env_harm_prevented × xᵢ)
Subject to: Σ(cost × xᵢ) ≤ Budget B
0/1 Knapsack · Greedy approximation · O(n log n)
